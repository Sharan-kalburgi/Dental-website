The Importance Of Oral Health

Author - Sharan
11 April | 1 min read

Oral health is an integral part of overall health and well-being.


Poor oral hygiene can lead to a variety of dental issues, such as cavities, gum disease, and tooth loss, which can impact your quality of life.


Regular brushing, flossing, and dental checkups are essential for maintaining good oral health and preventing more serious dental issues.
