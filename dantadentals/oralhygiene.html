Oral Hygiene

  • Good oral hygiene practices are crucial for maintaining healthy teeth and gums.
  • Brushing and flossing daily helps remove plaque and prevent cavities and gum disease.
  • A balanced diet and limited intake of sugary and acidic foods can also help keep your mouth healthy.
  • Regular dental checkups and cleanings can identify and treat issues early, leading to better oral health outcomes.
