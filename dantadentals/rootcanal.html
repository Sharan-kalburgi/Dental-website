Root Canal

Root canal treatment is a specialized dental procedure offered at Danta Dentals to treat infected or damaged teeth. Our skilled endodontists use state-of-the-art technology to remove the damaged pulp from the root canals, clean and shape them, and fill them with a biocompatible material to restore the tooth's function. The procedure is performed under local anesthesia to ensure the patient's comfort. With our expertise, you can trust us to save your natural teeth and restore your beautiful smile.
